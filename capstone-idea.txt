Story-plotting website. Where you can write down ideas, plot, characters, etc. You can keep them as simple or as fleshed-out as you want. 

MVP: 

1. Users can create account
2. Users can "Create New Book / Series"
3. Inside of books or series, users can create Characters and Worlds.
4. Inside of series, users can create multiple books. 
5. Inside of books, users can create chapters and input text including the plot. 
6. Users can add titles to books and series, names to characters and worlds, and their traits. 
7. Users can navigate between all books and series.
